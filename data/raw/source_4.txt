i got an offer in december. for me, both rounds were generic engineers I believe, but during my offer call, my recruiter told me he had already found a team for me, so I’m not sure how team matching works exactly. could be based on preferences form or just managers picking resumes they like.

i don’t think resume went too deep based on their questions, and it was mostly generic behavioural questions. however this could also be because i already went into a lot of detail myself whenever i mentioned things i did during past projects or internships as examples. honestly, the most important part is probably just to make interviewers like you.

programming question in technical interview was probably slightly harder side of leetcode easy. i think explaining your approach is always useful. also in my case it seemed like the interviewer liked talking to me about both my past experience and my questions about his work more, and we only spent like the last 10 minutes on the programming question as more of like a formality.
